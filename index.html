Quick guide:

Your story progress is currently saved only in your browser's local storage, as no login or signup is required to play.

If you'd like to save your progress or continue from where you left off after clearing browser data, or across different browsers or devices, here’s a step-by-step guide on how to do it.

Step 1: Before clearing your data or switching browsers/devices, remember the chapter number and the section you’re in (e.g., Chapter One – The Lost Boy).

Step 2: On your new browser/device, open the hamburger menu and go to the Chapters page.

Step 3: Under the Chapters section, select the chapter you were reading, then find the section name (sections are listed alphabetically).
Do not worry if you see section names you haven’t read yet — they are for different storylines.

Extra Tip: For more control, open the hamburger menu while reading. At the bottom-left corner of the side menu, you’ll see a page number. Copy that number and paste it into the Custom Jump field inside the chapter page to go directly to that exact spot.
